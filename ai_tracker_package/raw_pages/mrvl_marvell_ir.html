Company Overview

Marvell first revolutionized the digital storage industry by moving information at speeds never thought possible.

Today, Marvell optimized silicon powers AI, cloud, carrier and enterprise infrastructure. With a foundation built on breakthrough innovation, cutting-edge technology and deep system-level expertise, the networking, accelerated compute, storage, and security solutions developed by Marvell play a crucial role in enabling AI applications and driving advancements across industries.
